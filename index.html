Teach a new code

  1. Type the word or phrase this movement will mean, then click Record movement.
  2. Cover your face with one hand. Recording starts the moment you move it away.
  3. Perform your movement, then cover your face again. It saves by itself; no clicking needed.

The hand-to-face moments are trimmed automatically, so only the movement in between is saved. Record the same word more than once to add examples and make it easier to recognize.
